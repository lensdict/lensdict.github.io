Look up any word instantly — just point your camera at text. No typing, no internet required. Now with flash card sets and two-sides study mode!

How it works

Simply aim your camera at a book, article, menu, or sign. LensDict automatically detects the word at the center crosshair, highlights it, and shows the definition in a floating card. Tap the screen to pause and explore deeper.

📖

Powerful lookup

  • Two built-in dictionaries: WordNet 3.1 and Wiktionary — with automatic fallback if a word isn't found in one
  • Long-press any word in a definition to look it up (deep lookup)
  • Lemmatization recognizes word forms — "running" finds "run", "children" finds "child"
  • Text-to-speech pronunciation for every word
  • Works entirely offline — no internet connection required
💾

Save & organize

  • Bookmark words with one tap — each save captures a snapshot of what you were reading
  • Add personal notes with color coding to any saved word
  • Browse saved words with swipe navigation
